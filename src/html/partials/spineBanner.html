Spine

Spine is one of the most crucial and complex structure of the body. This is the only organ which can provide stability and flexibility to our body at the same time as it forms the core of our skeletal system. Because of its complexity, subtle changes in its structure can lead to major problems. It gives support to entire body, provides stability by transferring weight from torso to pelvis to legs. It is also a major shock absorbing structure and disc between two vertebral bodies is the most crucial structure providing shock absorption. Another crucial function is protection of spinal cords and nerves. Spinal cord traverses through the entire length of vertebral column.
