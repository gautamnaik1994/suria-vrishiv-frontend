Mind

The mind is the set of faculties including cognitive aspects such as consciousness, imagination, perception, thinking, intelligence, judgement, language and memory, as well as noncognitive aspects such as emotion and instinct.

The mind is closely linked to the brain, that is, the emotions, thoughts and cognitive processes are biological and regulated by neurochemicals in the brain. Just like other physical medical illnesses, mental illnesses have biological basis and need medical treatment too.

Mental health plays an important role for a healthy life as the mind has a huge impact on the functioning of the body (physical aspects).

Mental health, if affected, causes worsening in the existing medical illnesses or can also lead to development of physical ailments. If not treated timely, a mental illness can cause severe impairment in social and occupational functioning of an individual as it deals with the psychological(mind) as well as the biological(brain) aspects of a person.
